Privacy Policy

{% image './src/images/landing.png', 'Team Fuel Privacy Policy', '', 'lazy', '(max-width: 850px) 850px, 1920px' %}

Overview

Team Fuel respects your privacy. This notice explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices. This policy is US-focused, includes a simple CCPA “no sale” notice, and uses GDPR-friendly language.

Data We Collect

  • Forms: name, email, phone, company, project details
  • Analytics: page views, traffic sources, device/browser info (aggregated)
  • Server logs: IP address, timestamps, pages requested, errors

How We Use Data

  • Respond to inquiries and provide services you request
  • Improve our website and services via analytics
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse

Lawful Bases

  • Consent (e.g., when you submit forms)
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., site security, service improvement)

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) under agreements requiring appropriate safeguards.

Retention

  • Inquiry records: up to 24 months
  • Server logs: up to 12 months
  • Project records: retained as required to provide services and meet legal obligations

Your Rights

Subject to local law, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal data. To exercise rights, contact Dispatch@teamfueldenver.com.

Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies and analytics to understand site usage. You can adjust your browser settings to limit cookies. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No system is 100% secure.

CCPA Notice (California)

We do not sell personal information. California residents can contact us for access or deletion requests.

International Users

Our services are primarily US-focused. If you are outside the US, you consent to processing in the US.

Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Changes take effect when posted.
